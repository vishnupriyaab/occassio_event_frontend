Occassio - Terms and Conditions

1. Booking and Payment Policy
To confirm your event with Occassio, you are required to pay 1/3rd of the total estimated cost at the time of booking.
The remaining balance must be paid within 2 days after the event date.
A late payment fee of ₹50 per day will be charged for any overdue payments beyond this 2-day window.

2. Customization and Estimate Changes
Occassio allows event customization at any time before the event.
Any customization may affect the total cost, and your updated estimate will reflect those changes accordingly.

3. Cancellation and Refund Policy
The entry fee of ₹100 is non-refundable, under any circumstances.
Cancellations are not permitted within 3 days of the event date. No refund or rescheduling will be provided in such cases.

4. Client Responsibility
Clients are advised to plan and communicate their requirements clearly and well in advance.
Occassio is committed to delivering quality service, but the ultimate success of your event also depends on mutual cooperation and timely communication.

Disclaimer
Occassio reserves the right to update these terms and conditions at any time without prior notice.
Continued use of our services following any changes indicates acceptance of those changes.
